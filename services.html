Services

Drinks and Snacks

At Mangaka, we've curated a delightful selection of drinks and snacks to accompany your artistic journey or simply provide a moment of indulgence. Whether you're in the mood for a meticulously crafted espresso to fuel your inspiration or a delectable snack to satisfy your cravings, Mangaka Coffee Shop is your haven for artistic ambiance and culinary delight. Join us in exploring a world where your favorite manga characters come to life not only on the pages but also in every sip and nibble. Cheers to creativity, camaraderie, and the perfect blend of drinks and snacks at Mangaka!

Anime Art, Decor, Plushies and Figures

Enter the enthralling world of Mangaka Coffee Shop, where décor and anime art combine to provide an immersive experience for all fans. From lovingly exhibited figures of well-known characters to adorable plush toys that evoke fond memories. Mangaka Coffee Shop is more than just a cafe—whether you're an avid collector or just a casual fan—it's a refuge where fans of anime can immerse themselves in the charm and mystique of their beloved characters. Come enjoy this visual feast with us, where every aspect of our creative sanctuary will bring your love of anime to life!

Acoustic Entertainment

Our carefully chosen lineup of live events, which includes exceptional local performers, turns your visit into a blissful experience. Our acoustic entertainment adds a layer of warmth and joy to your time at the cafe. We have everything from heartfelt covers of your favorite anime theme songs to soulful renditions of those songs. Our acoustic sessions offer the ideal background for any activity, be it exploring the world of anime art, savoring delicious snacks, or sipping artisanal coffee. They create an atmosphere that is both humorous and melodic.
